WHAT'S NEXT?

What are our group members' expectations for next year in Information and Communications Technology 10?


I expect ICT 10 in OBMC to more complex than our previous topics in HTML, Cascading Styles Sheet, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I also hope that we will encounter new coding languages and create line-based games to further widen our perspectives and knowledges of the world of computer science and technology. Moreover, I hope that there will be more hands-on activities on our topics rather than relying on theoretical discussions in learning new programming languages. Furthermore, I hope that by the end of ICT 10, we can create a fully-fledged website and/or game with the programming techniques we will learn this upcoming school year!

Next year, I expect ICT to become more complicated. I think more coding languages would be introduced. There might also be some lessons about databases and how information is stored. Though I think by the end of the year we will know how to create a fully functioning website.

I expect ICT to have more difficult coding sessions and activities. We also might use different programming languages. I also expect that our wonderful ICT teacher of Grade 9, Mr. Luisito Balajadia, is still our teacher in Grade 10.

Grade 9 has been a tough year for me, but was also my best here in OB. I dont just expect some cool school projects and stuff from ICT, but I expect very great and close bonding with people that we'd be paired up or grouped with. Sure, OB has made a lot of students stressed because of school works, but its the people I met, the people that I was grouped with, that makes me want to continue on with school. Even in this summer, I just wished that I'd be able to hang out with the people I've met everyday like how it was in school. But then again, this summer was made as a break, but I see it as an opportunity for the future Grade 10, an opportunity to grow, and not stay idle.
